Global settings of \verb|width| and \verb|height| shall have an
impact on normal \verb|\includegraphics| but must not have an impact
on \verb|\includepdf|.

\verb|\setkeys{Gin}{width=3cm,height=3cm,keepaspectratio}|

The following picture will have a size of at most 3cm x 3cm.
But the \verb|\includepdf| following afterwards shall be full size.
\vspace*{\bigskipamount}


%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%
\setkeys{Gin}{width=3cm,height=3cm,keepaspectratio}

\includegraphics{example-image}

\includepdf[
  picturecommand={\put(80,650){\Large page shall be full size}}
]{dummy}

%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%%

Next pages with \verb|scale=0.8,frame| and some fance \verb|picturecommand|

\includepdfset{scale=0.8, frame,
     picturecommand={\put(80,600){%
         \Large Foobar \emph{test} {\bfseries XXX}
         \begin{tabular}[t]{ll}
           1&2\\3&4\\
         \end{tabular}
       }}}
\includepdf{dummy}
\includepdfmerge{dummy}

And normal again \ldots
